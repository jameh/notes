Deliverables¶

3 deliverables:
  • 5 weekly exercise sheets due weeks 2-6. For each exercise sheet that is “not adequate”, your final mark will be reduced by a factor of 0.02.
  • 30%: A project on a real dataset, report. Due week 11.
  • 70%: Final Exam - must pass for credit
    • a sheet of 8.5”×11” paper is allowed for reference on the Final.
